Team Name: Connect+

First Gen students face a myriad of challenges navigating college from financial barriers to emotional isolation to a lack of knowledge of the college experience. This project seeks to address these challenges by providing a resource (flutter app) for first gen students to get matched to resources, emotional support and advice, and opportunities for growth.
